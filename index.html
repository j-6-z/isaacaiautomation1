FAQ

Questions? We've Got Answers.

How quickly can we get started?
+
Most setups are completed within 1–2 weeks. We handle the full configuration — voice, scripting, and any basic integrations — so there's minimal lift on your end. You'll be live and answering calls before the month is out.
What kind of results should we expect?
+
Results vary by business, but the most common wins are: zero missed calls after hours, faster lead response times, and less time spent on repetitive phone tasks. We don't throw out inflated numbers — we let the AI speak for itself. That's why we offer a demo.
Do we need any technical knowledge?
+
None at all. You tell us how your business operates, what calls you get, and how you want them handled — we build and configure everything. You just answer calls (or let the AI handle them entirely).
Will this integrate with our current systems?
+
We're built on Retell AI, which supports integration with Google Calendar for appointment booking and can connect to a range of CRMs and tools via webhooks. Every setup is scoped during onboarding — if you use a specific tool, just ask us and we'll tell you straight up if it works.
What kinds of businesses do you work with?
+
We focus on trades and service businesses — plumbers, electricians, HVAC, contractors, and similar. If your phone is ringing and you can't always answer, we're built for you.
Is there a contract or commitment?
+
Monthly plans can be cancelled anytime. Annual plans are billed upfront at a discounted rate. No hidden fees — what you see on the pricing page is what you pay.

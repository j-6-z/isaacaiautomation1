Common Questions

How natural does it sound?
+
Incredibly natural. Most callers genuinely can't tell they're speaking with AI — the voice is fluid, context-aware, and handles interruptions gracefully.
What if a caller needs a real person?
+
The AI recognizes escalation cues and transfers seamlessly. You define the rules — urgent calls go straight through, everything else gets handled or scheduled.
Can it book real appointments?
+
Yes, fully. It connects to your Google Calendar or supported scheduling tools, checks availability in real time, and confirms bookings on the call.
What about after-hours calls?
+
That's where AI shines most. It works 24/7/365 with no overtime, no sick days, and no dropped calls at 2 AM.
Cost compared to a human receptionist?
+
Roughly 90% less. No salary, benefits, or training costs. It handles multiple calls simultaneously and never needs a break.
